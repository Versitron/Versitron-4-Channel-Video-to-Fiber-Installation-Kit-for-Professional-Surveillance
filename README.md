## What is a 4-Channel Video to Fiber Kit?

A 4-channel video to fiber kit converts multiple analog video feeds into optical signals for fiber optic transmission. By replacing traditional coaxial links with fiber, these kits enable extended cable runs with minimal signal loss and no electromagnetic interference. They are ideal for security, surveillance, and industrial video applications requiring high-quality, long-distance connectivity.
